PhD studies (2020-)

  • Weizmann Institute of Science, Rehovot, Israel

    Decomposing complex congition tasks like remembering people into individual, independent, simple tasks like face memory, face perception, general cogntion etc. The goal is then to relate those to brain activity using fMRI methods and use neurofeedback to tweak them.

    Group of Michal Ramot
